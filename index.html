Confirmed Rules

  • No yellow-card-only prize.
  • Most Cards is based on yellow cards and red cards combined.
  • No separate first red card prize.
  • Most Goals Conceded is across all of a person’s teams for the whole tournament.
  • Biggest Goal Difference Match is one single match, not total tournament goal difference.
  • If there is a tie for Most Goals Conceded, Most Cards, or Quickest Goal, the prize is split equally.
  • If multiple matches have the same biggest goal difference, the earliest match played wins.
  • If one person owns both teams in the Biggest Goal Difference Match, they receive the full £25.
